## Why This Approach Works

Traditional marketing often:
- Focuses only on features ("what it does")
- Targets only users OR developers
- Ignores the engineering process
- Treats DevOps as just CI/CD

This approach:
- Shows the complete lifecycle ("how it's built AND what it does")
- Speaks to multiple audiences simultaneously
- Demonstrates professional practices
- Treats DevOps as culture + practices + tools
